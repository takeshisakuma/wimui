## Best Practices

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Best Practice</th>
      <th>Description</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Use Sparingly</strong></td>
      <td>Only show loading overlays when necessary to avoid disrupting the user experience.</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Provide Context</strong></td>
      <td>Include a message when the loading operation might take more than a few seconds.</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Choose Appropriate Scope</strong></td>
      <td>Use absolute positioning for partial page updates and fixed positioning for full-page operations.</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Consider Backdrop</strong></td>
      <td>Use light backdrops on dark backgrounds and vice versa for better visibility.</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Timeout Handling</strong></td>
      <td>Always implement timeout handling to prevent indefinite loading states.</td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

## Accessibility

- The component includes `role="status"` and `aria-live="polite"` for screen reader announcements.
- The `aria-busy="true"` attribute indicates that the content is loading.
- Ensure that loading messages are descriptive and helpful for users relying on assistive technologies.
